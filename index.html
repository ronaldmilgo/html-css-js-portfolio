    Hi—I’m Ronald Milgo. I’m currently pursuing a BS in Computer Science at Yale, where I’ve spent countless hours bringing web ideas to life from both ends of the stack. On the frontend, I craft responsive UIs with React, Tailwind, and accessible design patterns; on the backend, I’ve built RESTful APIs in Flask and Node, wired up SQL and NoSQL databases, and automated deployments to AWS and Docker. Beyond academics, I’ve cut my teeth at early-stage startups like Neotix AI—where I helped design and implement the robotics “Scale AI” data platform, from edge-ML pose-tracking on VisionLite camera kits to the cloud pipeline that ingests terabytes of tele-operated sensor streams. Whether I’m sketching wireframes, debugging a CORS issue, or architecting hardware-in-the-loop data systems, I thrive on turning ambitious tech visions into working reality—and mentoring others as I go.
